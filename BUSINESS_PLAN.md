## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### The Opportunity

The marketing automation software market is valued at **$5.2 billion** (2025) and projected to reach **$13.7 billion by 2030** (CAGR 21.4%). Despite this growth, customers consistently report frustration with:

- Complex, opaque pricing that escalates unpredictably
- Feature bloat that creates steep learning curves
- Vendor lock-in with difficult data portability
- Poor integration between tools
- Lack of transparency in how their data is used

### The Solution

**MARKTOS** is a 25-layer Marketing Genome Platform built on **ValidKernel™** - a Deterministic Trust Infrastructure that makes trust visible, auditable, and verifiable. Unlike competitors, MARKTOS offers:

- **Zero-build architecture** - Single HTML file, no dependencies
- **Offline-first** - Works without internet, no cloud lock-in
- **VTI™ enabled** - Verifiable Trust Infrastructure for compliance
- **Transparent pricing** - Trust Class tiers with clear feature access
- **Self-hosted option** - Complete data ownership

**Use of Funds:**

- Operations (15%)
- Legal/IP (10%)
